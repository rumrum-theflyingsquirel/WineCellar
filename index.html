Francis Trudeau

Owner of the T.O.T. Cellar

I recently discover my passion for wine so during the spring quarantaine, I build a wonderful cellar in my basement. It was in a lost space in my workshop so I thought it would be great to have a nice place to put my wine. So in one week, I transform an old cold room into a hundred and more wine bottle cellar. From this day, Me and my wife try to resist the urge of drinking all these good bottles because was is the point to have a nice cellar if there is nothing in it.
